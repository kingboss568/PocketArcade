Pocket Arcade Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-05

Pocket Arcade is designed as a local-first offline arcade collection.

Data Stored On Device

The app stores game progress, high scores, unlock flags, sound settings, and onboarding state locally using SwiftData and UserDefaults.

Purchases

StoreKit 2 is used for non-consumable purchases. Apple processes purchase transactions. The app reads verified entitlements to unlock games and remove ads.

Game Center

If the player signs into Game Center, high scores may be submitted to Apple Game Center leaderboards.

Ads

The current build only contains an ad provider protocol and does not bundle GoogleMobileAds. A future build may add rewarded ads. If added, ads will be disabled or hidden when the device is offline.

AI

Arcade Coach uses local RAG evidence and may use Apple Foundation Models in a future supported build. If unavailable, the app displays "AI 功能目前不可用". AI output is auxiliary entertainment guidance only and does not affect scoring, purchases, or progress.
